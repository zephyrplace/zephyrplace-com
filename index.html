Simple. Fast. Delivery.

Developing a minimum viable product (MVP) that solves your problem to begin the process of learning as quickly as possible. Once the MVP is established, you can go forward. This will involve measurement and learning and must include actionable metrics that can demonstrate cause and effect question.

The Basics Steps

1. Discovery how to

The first step is figuring out the problem that needs to be solved.

2. Develop a MVP

An MVP which is a product with a minimum set of features that solves a definite problem.

3. Establish the MVP

By the time that product is ready to be distributed widely, it will already have established customers.

4. Measure your results

Measuring customers' reactions and behaviors against built products, and then deciding whether to persevere or pivot the idea.

5. Learn with

The goal of an MVP is cheapest way to test your solution and help to begin the learning process as quickly as possible.

6. Evolve your solution

Scale to market and attract more users, know you can really invest in a tested and validated idea.

Build. Measure. Learn.
